For a hidden layer:
1. Choose the number of neurons
2. Determine the shape of the weight_matrix
#rows = #neurons in the hidden layer
#cols = #neurons in the previous layer 
3. Choose values for the connection weights (in other words, populate the weight matrix)
4. Compute weighted sums for each neuron, neuron i = weight_matrix[at row_i]*previous_layer_vector
5. Pass weighted sums to the non-linear function. Here we use Relu: y = max(0,x)
6. Values from step 5 are the values for our hidden layer neurons
